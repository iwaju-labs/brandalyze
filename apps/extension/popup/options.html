Getting Started

How to Use Brandalyze

  1. Sign in - Connect your Brandalyze Pro+ account to enable all features
  2. Set up your handle - Add your Twitter/X username in Social Media Handles
  3. Navigate to a profile - Go to your profile page
  4. Analyze profiles - Click the "Analyze Profile" button that appears on profile pages
  5. Audit your posts - When composing a post, click the Brandalyze button to check brand alignment before posting

Features

  • Profile Analysis - Analyze any Twitter/X or LinkedIn profile for brand voice characteristics
  • Post Auditing - Check your drafts for brand alignment before posting
  • Emotional Indicators - Track specific emotional tones in your content
  • History - View past analyses and audits on brandalyze.io/history

Pro Tips

  • Use post auditing to maintain consistent messaging
  • Select emotional indicators that match your brand personality
