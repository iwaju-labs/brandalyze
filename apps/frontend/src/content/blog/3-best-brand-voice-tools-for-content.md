This is a totally understandable, yet preventable problem, especially in today's era of technology. ## Tool 1: Brand Voice Checker
### What is the Brand Voice Checker?
The brand voice checker is a simple yet effective tool. You paste a sample of your content (e.g. social media posts, blogs, marketing copy, emails etc), and the tool will analyze your brand voice and generate a detailed report categorizing it so you know how you sound to your audience.

use it for free [here](https://brandalyze.io/brand-voice-checker)
