Many brands struggle with content becoming inconsistent across social media, blogs, and marketing copy. This is a totally understandable, yet preventable problem, especially in today's era of technology. In this blog I will introduce you to 3 free tools you can use to get a better idea of/start improving your brand voice today.

## Tool 1: Brand Voice Checker
The brand voice checker is a simple yet effective tool. You paste a sample of your content (e.g. social media posts, blogs, marketing copy, emails etc), and the tool will analyze your brand voice and generate a detailed report categorizing it so you know how you sound to your audience.
