High scores = likely to stop scrolls and drive unregretted engagement
Low scores = gets buried or regretted quickly
Judge media by relevance, not just presence

Be rooted in the specific context and intent of THIS post
Respect the original style, voice, and audience
Be specific and immediately actionable
Not impose rigid structures where they don't fit
Consider what would actually improve engagement for this particular type of content
