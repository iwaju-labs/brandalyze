# cold outreach

### best practices
- be personal
- short messages (value-focus, not copy and paste)
- offer something of value up front: insights, free audit, case study, demo

- use posts/reach to build credibility

### Example outreach sequence

- Email/LinkedIn message: 
  - “Hi [Name], I noticed you’re heading brand/marketing at [Company]. Many SaaS companies we speak to say that ‘brand sentiment analytics’ is a blind spot. We built brandalyze.io to fill that gap (cutting analysis time by 50 % for [Client]). Would love to send a 15-min demo or a free brand sentiment snapshot for your brand if you’re interested. Best, [Your name]”.

- Follow-up (3-5 days later): 
  - “Hi [Name], Just checking in — if you’re curious, here’s a 2-min video showing how brandalyze works [link]. Would next Tuesday at 10 am work for a quick call?”

- Final touch (after another ~5-7 days): 
  - “Hi [Name], I understand your plate is full. If easier I’d be happy to provide a self-serve snapshot — no obligations. Let me know if you’d like me to set that up.”

- Optional: If no response, send value email (case study or insights). “We analysed 150 SaaS brands: this one KPI predicts brand growth…”
