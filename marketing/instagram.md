# instagram

### algorithm and best practices
- use interactive formats: posts, stories, reels, carousels
- high impact hashtags
- be clear in captions (dont make them too long)
- CTA (link in bio, Comment "BRAND" and I'll dm you the link, etc)
- Speed of engagement matters. need people to like, comment, share in the first few hours of upload

### approach
- create reels showcasing brandalyze
- look into UGC
- carousel posts giving tips on brand monitoring/analysis
- behind the scenes personal updates (facing the camera)
