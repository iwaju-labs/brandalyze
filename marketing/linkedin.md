# linkedin

### algorithm and best practices
- focus on value, not hard sales &mdash; insights not just product demos
- variety of posts, videos, images, carousels
- prioritise early engagement
- 3-5 hashtags per post (dont exceed)
- engage with others

### tactics for posts
- make posts that address engaging topics like *"5 Mistakes Companies Make in Brand Analytics"* &rightarrow; then use brandalyze as a potential solution
- create carousel dekcs eg. "Brand sentiment from data to action"
- posts like "What I learned using Brandalyze to analyze my social media"

### posting cadence
- quality over quantity
- mix use of article, post, carousel, video styles
- always include a call to action (CTA) - "Get Started for Free"
